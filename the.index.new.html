Welcome

When all other burrito franchises claim a commitment to freshness and quality, what is it that places Jumbo Burrito in a league of its own?

The answer is simple.

While they pre-cook their chicken breast and steaks for hours before they are served, we, at Jumbo Burrito, take the time to grill each order to perfection at the time it is placed. Furthermore, while they toast their burritos on flat top grills, which sometime burns the tortillas, we, at Jumbo Burrito, toast each burrito for a pre-set time on a pressure sensitive, state of the art, pre-heated grill. This allows enough time to thoroughly and evenly heat the inner burrito, which leaves each tortilla golden crispy and ready to be savoured.

What does all this add up to?

You, the customer, are guaranteed total freshness, and the highest quality and best tasting burrito or quesadilla possible!

In other words, "There is no doubt about it, our burrito can't be made to taste any better!"
